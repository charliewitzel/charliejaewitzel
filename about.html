Drag King

Step into the mesmerizing world of Silas Silence, a non-binary transmasculine drag performer who weaves horror and gender-bending artistry into captivating performances. Known for their spine-chilling portrayals of horror icons like Freddy, Jason, and Chucky, Silas Silence has taken the stage by storm. As the 2016 King of Pride in St. Louis, MO, they have solidified their place as a trailblazer in the drag community. With a knack for incorporating SFX makeup and a flair for gender-bending numbers, they transcend boundaries and captivate audiences with their unique blend of theatrics, dance, and storytelling. Silas Silence is a beacon of empowerment, inspiring others to embrace their true selves and showcasing the beauty of self-expression. Prepare to be spellbound as they transport you to a world where horror and gender fluidity intertwine, leaving an indelible mark on your heart and mind.
